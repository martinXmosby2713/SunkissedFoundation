The Sun Kissed Foundation (SKF) in Los Angeles, California. showcases the history, art, and cultural richness that resulted from the African diaspora due to the migration of Africans throughout the world. Our mission here at SKF is to highlight Historically Black College Universities (HBCU's) by purchasing and donating artworks from their shared African heritage, paintings and sculptures that reflect the diversity of the hueman experience. SKF hosts exhibits/auctions where connoisseurs of art and diverse culture can bid for artwork for the purpose of philanthropy.
